Snomed2Vec, is a new approach to use Spanish Snomed-CT.

You can get the most similar clincial concept of a text, from Snomed-CT.
We use word embedding from the description Terms of the Spanish Snomed-CT, using Word2Vec framework.
We train a space vector model with two kind of big Corpus. Local domain with emergency discharges reports and general domain with Wikipedia.
The final Snomed2Vec model, it's develope with the idConcept, Term, hierachy and Vector of the descriptions Term from the Spanish Snomed-CT.
To identify the most similar concepts in our model with use the cosine distance similarity from the vector Space Model Snomed2Vec.

Try it, and Test-tab, to find the most similar Spanish Snomed-CT concepts, from a text.
